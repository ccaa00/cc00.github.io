Ewant Open Education Platform

Overview

Ewant Open Education Platform was established in 2013 as the first Massive Open Online Courses (MOOCs) platform in Taiwan. This platform was designed based on Moodle, an open-source online learning system. We aimed to provide a course demonstration and management site for all MOOCs partners, and to offer more learning opportunities through free online courses to whoever is interested in continuous learning.

According to the trend of Open Online Courses (MOOCs) around the world, open-source online learning was inevitable. However, most of the MOOCs were in English, and there was not a platform in Mandarin Chinese. Therefore, we wanted to establish our own MOOCs platform. For this objective, we referred the courses on our Open Course Ware (National Chiao Tung University OCW), an open online course providing self-study resources and knowledge of courses in the university by the university. And, we designed and built the MOOCs platform for all the public, not just only for the university students. We hoped we could bring education to everyone, everywhere, and every moment.
