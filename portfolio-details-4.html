Tangerine Charity Series Project

Overview

Industrial Technology Research Institute (ITRI) is a world-leading applied technology research institute with more than 6,000 outstanding employees. Its mission is to drive industrial development, create economic value, and enhance social well-being through technology R&D. In response to global social responsibility development trends, they leverage their research results to promote public welfare. As a social enterprise, we had the same vision to fulfill the mission of "using technical R&D to stimulate industry development, create economic benefits, and increase public welfare". And, that was the spark of this project.

Tangerine is a fruit that ripens in the winter in Taiwan, with the good meaning of its name that sounds similar as “Lucky,” also their picking timing is usually near to Chinese New Year. Tangerines are popular around Chinese New Year. However, most of the farmers are the elders, and only know how to grow good fruits but not marketing and selling. Starting from this issue, we contacted local elder farmers, and told them we were going to buy all their quality fruits with the price higher than the market, and sell in fruit gift boxes. Then, we used ICT tools adding the value of the fruits, like Halftone Quick Response Code (Halftone QR Code) that you can directly see the grower’s face on the QR Code and growing record after you scan it. Over 50 companies and hundreds of individual publics joined this series project each year. We did not stop here. Beginning from the next year, not only tangerine gift box, but we partnered with social enterprises producing superior quality stuff to launch new year gift boxes. All the net profits were donated to qualified charities in the name of each customer. Furthermore, we invited disadvantaged children to attend our designed innovation activities, such as tangerine picking experience, tangerine chocolates making, eco-friendly detergent making, mountain-related knowledge challenge activities, professional occupations sharing.
